Welcome to the professional conditory course!

A premium track that provides comprehensive knowledge in the field of confectionery while constantly being exposed to international trends.
The track encompasses all the basics of patisserie and baking confectionery and includes classic confectionery, the track combines intensive practical work throughout all stages of learning. The work is carried out in well-equipped workstations as an integral part of the acquisition of the profession.
At the end of the track, you can join the confectionery field.
